What is KucoCoin?

KucoCoin is an ERC20 token deployed on the Ethereum network. Kuco means "love and prosper" in Nigerian. In contrast to many other tokens, KucoCoin is not a meme token. Attaining stability

KucoCoin revolutionizes the market by introducing a blockade on transactions at any time chosen by a decentralized validation process controlled by the centralized token owner. Blocking every single KUCO transaction means no exchange swapping can happen at that time. This means no price change of the KUCO is possible, as it can be neither bought nor sold. As a result, KUCO, can be viewed as a stablecoin sometimes.

Zero-knowledge capabilities

KucoCoin believes in zero-knowledge-proof technology. To show support we prove to have zero knowledge of this technology by implementing a system that allows users to use zero-knowledge-proofs with KucoCoin. Our contract provides a function `acceptZK` which accepts a totally unrestricted bytes object and emits an event that it has been provided. That object can be anything you want it to be, it can be a ZK-snark, ZK-stark, or a utf8-encoded message "I love KucoCoin!"". The possibilities are endless, just like your love for KucoCoin!
