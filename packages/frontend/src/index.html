Kuconomics - the distribution model

Below we describe the tokenomics of KUCO distribution. The model assumes a fixed Uniswap-V2 liquidiy pool, denoted LP, that collects AVAX for an initially fixed deposit of newly minted KUCO tokens. Before reading the following scheme, note that you should try to understand the scheme in the positive light, without negative judgement, in the way we want you to.

1. Investing AVAX liquidity

The liquidity pool LP has an initial deposit of a billion KUCO tokens, with deposit's liquidity burned. Before KucoCoin can be traded, users can invest in the pool, by providing AVAX as liquidity, which is then burned. Investment is not refundable, though it can be retracted for 10% fee.

2. Collecting minted KUCO

After some time, KUCO will be minted to the accounts of investors who deposited AVAX to the liquidity pool LP. If at that time the liquidity in LP is comprised of K KUCO and A AVAX, with you investing a AVAX, then you will receive 1.2 × a × K / A KUCO in return.
